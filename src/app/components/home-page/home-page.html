Our Story

Founded in 2024, BYR was born from a desire to share timeless family recipes with the world. Our journey began with a small kitchen and a big dream: to create a place where every meal tells a story. We believe in the magic of fresh ingredients, the warmth of hospitality, and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones.
